Conjuring Up the Deviled Eggs

This easy deviled eggs recipe is delicious, and it's simple to make more for larger gatherings.

Ingredients

  • 6 hard-boile eggs
  • 2 tablespoons mayonnaise
  • 1 teaspoon white sugar
  • 1 teaspoon white vinegar
  • 1 teaspoon prepared mustard
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 tablespoon finely chopped onion
  • 1 tablespoon finely chopped celery
  • 1 pinch paprika

Steps

  1. Hard boil 6 eggs.
  2. Slice eggs in half lengthwise and remove yolks; set whites aside.
  3. Mash yolks with a fork in a small bowl. Stir in mayonnaise, sugar, vinegar, mustard, salt, onion, and celery; mix well.
  4. Stuff or pipe egg yolk mixture into egg whites.
  5. Sprinkle with paprika and refrigerate until serving.
