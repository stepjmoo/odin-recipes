Green Bean Casserole

Throwing Together A Savory Green Bean Casserole

A green bean casserole makes the Thanksgiving holiday complete. Made with just four budget-friendly ingredients, you'll come back to this green bean casserole recipe year after year.

Ingredients

  • 2 drained cans of green beans
  • 1 can of condensed cream of mushroom soup
  • shredded cheddar cheese (your preference of amount and whether mild or sharp)
  • French-fried onions (enough to cover the dish)
  • Optional Ingredients:crumbled bacon, sauteed mushrooms, garlic

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
  2. Mix the beans and soup in a microwave-safe bowl and microwave until warm.
  3. Stir in half the cheese. Microwave until melted and well-blended.
  4. Transfer to a prepared baking dish. Top with fried onions and remaining cheese.
  5. Bake in the preheated oven for 10 minutes or until the cheese is melted and the onions are brown.
